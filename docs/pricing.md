# Pricing & Sales Guide

## Build Cost Breakdown
- RCGF 10cc Engine: ~$180
- Pixhawk Flight Controller: ~$200-300
- Airframe/Materials: ~$300-400
- Servos, ESCs, Receivers: ~$150-200
- Sensors (GPS, RPM, etc.): ~$100-150
- Misc (fuel system, wiring, hardware): ~$100-150
- **Total Build Cost: ~$1,100-1,300**

## Labor & Testing
- Assembly: 10-20 hours
- Testing/Tuning: 5-10 hours
- Documentation: 2-5 hours

## Suggested Retail Pricing

| Market Segment | Price Range | Includes |
|----------------|-------------|----------|
| Kit Only (unassembled) | $1,800-2,200 | Parts, instructions, params |
| Hobbyist (assembled, untested) | $2,500-3,500 | Assembled, basic config |
| Small Business/Farm | $4,000-6,000 | Tested, tuned, training |
| Professional/Commercial | $6,000-10,000 | Full support, warranty, custom config |

## Value Propositions
- 1-2+ hour flight endurance (vs 30 min electric)
- Full ArduPilot autonomous capability
- Pre-configured and flight-tested
- Documentation and support included

## Warranty Options
- 30-day: Included in Professional tier
- 90-day: +$300
- 1-year: +$800 (parts only)

## Competition Analysis
- DJI Matrice 300: $10,000-15,000
- AgEagle eBee: $12,000+
- Custom gas fixed-wing: $5,000-15,000
